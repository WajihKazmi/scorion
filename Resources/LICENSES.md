# Scorion Factory Content & Third-Party DSP Notes

## Factory content (owned by Scorion / ATech Solution)

All files under `Resources/factory/` are **original**:

- Wavetables generated by `scripts/gen_wavetables.py` (band-limited harmonic morphs, formants, metallic spectra). Format: `.wtbin` (float32 frames) + preview `.wav`.
- Presets authored for Scorion (JSON).
- Builtin sampler/granular tones are procedurally synthesized at runtime.

You may ship them with Scorion. Do not claim they are third-party commercial banks.

## Roadmap toward Serum / Omnisphere class

Beating Serum/Omnisphere is a multi-year product goal. Current production upgrades:

1. Mipmapped wavetable oscillator + factory 256-frame tables
2. Dual filter path (TPT SVF + Moog ladder)
3. 2x oversampled saturation, chorus, FDN reverb, HP feedback delay
4. Original cinematic / evolving soundscape preset bank

Next: table browser UI, true ADAA oscillators, convolution IR hall, multi-layer sampler zones.
